Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Fire-RMS cost?

We offer simple, transparent pricing: $25 per member per year for volunteer fire departments, and $100 per member per year for career departments. Early adopters receive special benefits including free trials and significant discounts.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Fire-RMS is built with a mobile-first approach, providing a seamless experience on smartphones, tablets, and computers.

Can it integrate with our current systems?

Yes! Drawing on years of experience with public safety integrations, Fire-RMS is designed to connect with CAD systems, EMS platforms, and other department software.

Is Fire-RMS ready for the 2026 standards?

Absolutely. Fire-RMS is built from the ground up as a NERIS-compliant system, ready for the 2026 transition while maintaining backward compatibility with legacy NFIRS where needed.
